# Provincial Government of Ilocos Norte

## About the Province
The **Provincial Government of Ilocos Norte** is the primary governing body of the province of Ilocos Norte, committed to upholding good governance, transparency, and inclusive development. It serves as the driving force in implementing programs and policies that promote the social, economic, and cultural progress of the Ilokano people.

## Mission
To deliver efficient, transparent, and people-centered public services through accountable governance, innovation, and strong partnerships, ensuring sustainable development and improved quality of life for all Ilocos Norteños.

## Vision
A progressive, resilient, and globally competitive Ilocos Norte, guided by principled leadership, empowered communities, and sustainable development.

## Core Values
- **Integrity** – Upholding ethical standards and accountability in public service  
- **Transparency** – Ensuring openness in governance and decision-making  
- **Public Service** – Prioritizing the welfare and needs of the people  
- **Excellence** – Delivering efficient, responsive, and high-quality services  
- **Unity** – Fostering collaboration among government, private sector, and communities  
- **Innovation** – Embracing technology and modern solutions for effective governance  

## Key Functions
- Formulate and implement provincial policies, plans, and programs  
- Deliver basic and specialized services to cities, municipalities, and barangays  
- Promote economic growth, tourism, agriculture, and sustainable development  
- Ensure disaster preparedness, climate resilience, and public safety  
- Manage provincial resources with accountability and fiscal responsibility  

## Commitment to Ilocos Norteños
The Provincial Government of Ilocos Norte is committed to strengthening public trust through participatory governance, digital transformation, and responsive leadership. We continuously strive to create opportunities, protect cultural heritage, and build a better, more inclusive future for every Ilocos Norteño.
